University of California, Los Angeles

The UCLA chapter was our first chapter that helped shaped our organization and the work we do. They focus on working with the villagers of Vadamanappakkam, located in rural Tamil Nadu and have had a significant impact on this community. This chapter has a strong focus on improving quality of life through various health initiatives.
