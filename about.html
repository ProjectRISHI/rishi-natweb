About Us


Project RISHI - Rural India Social and Health Improvement - is a mission-driven non profit committed to fostering the growth and development of rural India. We are diverse group of passionate energetic individuals moving forward the dialogue on equity and reilience through our projects.


Our Mission


Our mission is to promote the sustainable development of rural Indian communities. In partnership with local community members and social enterprises, we identify issues central to our target communities and provide the resources to implement solutions through extensive field research and on-campus initiatives.

Our Team


We are a team of entrepreneurs, educators, business leaders, developers, students, and more who believe deeply in the power of innovation and initiative to improve social and health conditions in rural India.
