Privacy Policy


Project RISHI uses GOOGLE ANALYTICS on ProjectRISHI.org

GOOGLE ANALYTICS is a Google Product that offers data about our visitors. GOOGLE ANALYTICS tracks a visitor's acquisition channels, length of a visit, platform/browser used, and some behavioral data based on your interaction with the site. The tracking information allows us to better understand our visitors, which helps us make better decisions about our offerings and design.


How will we use this Data?

Occasionally, Project RISHI will aggregate statistics about the number of visitors the site receives and their overall interaction. No personal identifying data is included in this type of reporting.


Want to opt out of Tracking?

1. To opt out of Google's advertising tracking cookie: Google Privacy and Terms
2. Use a Browser Plugin: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Plugin


All of Project RISHI Google Analytics activity falls within the bounds of GOOGLE ANALYTICS TERMS OF SERVICE.
